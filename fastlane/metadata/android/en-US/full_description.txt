Buskina is an open-source transit navigation app for cities in Burkina Faso.

Find bus routes, browse stops, and plan trips across Ouagadougou and Bobo-Dioulasso — with offline support so it works without a data connection.

Features:
* Search itineraries between any two stops
* Browse all lines and stops in your city
* Offline routing — no internet required once data is cached
* Save favourite stops, lines, and itineraries
* Bus stop locations sourced from OpenStreetMap

The app loads the Buskina progressive web app (https://buskina.com) in a native WebView with geolocation support. The full source code — including the routing server, GTFS pipeline, and frontend — is available at https://github.com/xacobe/buskina
